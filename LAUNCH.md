## 📝 Blog Post Draft: "Why Your Team's AI Stack is a Financial Mess"

**The AI sprawl is real.**

In 2023, the goal was "get AI tools into the hands of developers."
In 2024, the goal is "stop paying for 4 different LLM subscriptions for the same 10 people."

We built **Spendora** to solve the "AI Shadow IT" problem.

### The Problem with Subscriptions
Most AI vendors (OpenAI, Anthropic, Perplexity) default to $20-30/user/month. When a developer has 3 of these plus a coding assistant, you're spending $100+/mo per seat. 

Multiply that by a 50-person team, and you're at $60k/year without even looking at your API credits.

### How Spendora Audits Your Stack
Spendora uses a deterministic engine to map tool capabilities. It doesn't just guess; it knows that if you have Claude Pro and ChatGPT Team, you probably have a capability overlap in "Reasoning & Writing."

It highlights:
1. **Unused Seats**: Paying for 20 seats but only 12 people on the team? That's $160/mo back in your pocket.
2. **API vs. Subscription**: Heavy researcher? Keep the sub. Occasional user? Move to the API and save 80%.
3. **Consolidation**: Do you really need Cursor AND Copilot? (Hint: Usually no).

### Get Benchmarked
With our new **Benchmark Mode**, you can see where your stack sits relative to other engineering teams. Are you "over-tooled"? Is your spend per developer in the top 10%?
