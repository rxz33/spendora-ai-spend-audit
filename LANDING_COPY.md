## FAQ (5 Questions)

### 1. Do I have to sign up to see results?
**Answer:**
No. Run the audit first, see your results on-screen, and *then* decide if you want to capture them. Value before email — always.

### 2. How accurate are the savings estimates?
**Answer:**
Based on official pricing from vendor websites, verified on the submission date. Each recommendation links to the exact pricing page we used. We also tell you when savings are <$100/month (probably not worth switching effort) and when your spend is already optimized.

### 3. Can I share the audit with my team?
**Answer:**
Yes. Each audit gets a unique shareable link. Safe to share in Slack or email — no sensitive data included, just recommendations and savings numbers.

### 4. What if I don't have any savings opportunities?
**Answer:**
We tell you honestly. No fake savings or exaggerated claims. If your spending is healthy, we say so — and offer to notify you when new optimizations apply to your stack.

### 5. Who is behind Spendora?
**Answer:**
Spendora is built by the Credex team. Credex buys discounted AI infrastructure credits from companies that overforecast. We built this tool to help teams optimize *before* partnering with us — it's better for everyone.
