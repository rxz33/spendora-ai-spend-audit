# Unit Economics — Spendora for Credex

## Per-Audit Lead Value

### Assumptions
- Average audit savings: **$280/month**
- High-savings threshold: >$500/month (~50% of audits)
- Avg high-savings amount: **$850/month**

### Conversion Funnel

```
100 Audits Completed
↓
60 Emails Captured (60% conversion rate)
↓
30 Read Credex CTA (50% CTR)
↓
6 Book Consultation (20%)
↓
2 Sign Contracts (33% close rate)
```

### Lead Value Calculation

- **Credex margin:** 40% (typical SaaS margin on credits)
- **Annual savings per customer:** $850/mo × 12 = **$10,200**
- **Revenue per contract:** $10,200 × 40% = **$4,080**
- **LTV (3-year, 10% churn):** $4,080 × 2.7 years = **$11,000**

**Per-audit value to Credex:** $11,000 ÷ 50 audits = **~$220/audit**
