# Unit Economics — Spendora as Credex Lead-Gen Asset

## Lead Value Calculation

### Assumptions
- **Average audit discovers savings:** $280/month
- **High-savings threshold:** >$500/month (50% of audits)
- **Avg high-savings amount:** $850/month

### Lead Conversion Path
```
Audit Completed (100 leads)
↓
Email Captured (60 leads, 60% conversion)
↓
Opens Credex CTA (30 leads, 50% read CTA)
↓
Books Credex Consultation (6 leads, 20% CTR)
↓
Signs Credex Contract (2 leads, 33% close rate)
```


### Lead Value to Credex
- **Credex margin on annual credits:** ~40% (typical SaaS margin)
- **Annual savings per customer:** $850/month × 12 = $10,200
- **Credex take per contract:** $10,200 × 40% = **$4,080**
- **LTV (3-year retention, annual churn 10%):** $4,080 × 2.7 years ≈ **$11,000**

**Per-audit value to Credex:** $11,000 ÷ 50 audits per 1 conversion = **~$220 per audit**

## Key Economic Triggers

**When to scale paid ads:**
- Month 2: If organic CAC is <$50
- Month 3: Once product has 300+ audits and engagement data

**When to hire:**
- Month 6: If hitting $1K+ audits/week, hire 1 customer success person
- Month 12: If at $50K+/month revenue, hire product + marketing

**When to pivot:**
- If conversion audit→consultation <10% after Month 4
- If lead quality drops (audits with <$100 savings increase above 60%)
